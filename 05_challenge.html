The Red Panda

The red panda is awesome because red pandas have extremely flexible ankles,
they are one of the few animals that can climb down trees head-first.
The red panda, also known as the lesser panda,
is a small mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China.
It has dense reddish-brown fur with a black belly and legs, white-lined ears,
a mostly white muzzle and a ringed tail
