Egusi Soup

Recipe Ingredients Steps
Egusi Soup
  • Ground melon seeds (egusi)
  • spinach or bitterleaf
  • palm oil
  • meat (beef/goat/fish)
  • crayfish
  • Stock fish
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  1. Fry egusi in palm oil
  2. add onions and crayfish
  3. Add meat and stock
  4. then simmer
  5. Add vegetables and cook until flavors meld.
  6. Add prawns or shrimp for a seafood twist. Serve with fufu or pounded yam

Jollof Rice

Recipe Ingredients Steps
Jollof Rice
  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chiken (optional)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Thyme curry
  • Bay leaves
  1. Blend tomatoes peppers, and onions; cook in oil until reduced.
  2. Add seasoning, thyme, and curry.
  3. Add rice and broth.
  4. Simmer until rice is tender.
  5. For smoky flavor, cook on low heat and let the rice at the bottom slightly caramelize.

Moi Moi

Recipe Ingredients Steps
Moi Moi
  • Bean
  • Cray fish
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  1. Blend soaked beans with peppers and onions.
  2. Add oil, crayfish, and seasoning.
  3. Steam in bowls or wraps until set and firm
  4. Add boiled eggs or fish in the center for variation.

Akara

Recipe Ingredients Steps
Akara
  • Black-eyed beans
  • Salt
  • scotch bonnet pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  1. Blend peeled beans with onions and peppers
  2. Add seasoning, thyme, and curry
  3. Season with salt
  4. then fry small spoonfuls in hot oil until golden brown
  5. Serve as breakfast or snack with pap (ogi) or bread

Bitterleaf Soup

Recipe Ingredients Steps
Biterleaf Soup
  • cocoyam paste
  • Bitterleaf
  • palm oil
  • Dry fish
  • Assorted meat
  • seasoning cubes
  • Crayfish
  1. Boil meat and dry fish with seasonings
  2. Add palm oil, cocoyam paste as thickener, and washed bitterleaf
  3. Cook until well blended.
  4. Substitute spinach if bitterleaf is unavailable
  5. Best served with pounded yam
